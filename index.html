I discovered the wonderous world of IT when I was still a child. I've spent countless hours playing Little Big Planet, Killzone 3, GTA Vice city, Minecraft and that is to name a few. But it wasn't untill I was 12 years old untill I fell in love with it. I just got into my dream school in my town and I met my mentor. She was and still is one of my biggest supporters and inspiration. She made me realise that I can achive something with just my imagination, thinking and a computer.

Then a few months later I was with my father and he suggested showing me how to make calculator. So we did a calculator on c++. It was my greatest achivement in that moment and I still cherish the memory of it (even though the calculator was made with integers and when you divided 5 by 2 the result was 2). My mentor and my dad motivated me to learn the basics of Visual Basic, then of HTML and CSS. After that my journey continued with C#, SQL and more. One of my proudest moments was the moments where I had been a substitute CS teacher and I loved seeing the faces of my fellow classmates when they understood what I had explained. So even though I haven't decided if I want to become a developer or a teacher I know that my place in the world is somewhere within the IT field.
