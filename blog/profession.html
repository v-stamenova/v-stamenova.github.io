Introduction

Searching for your dream position and company is a long and difficult search full of unexpected things. Here I am listing my top 3 companies that I would like to work as a part of the software engineering team. These companies are companies that work with the language C#, and I have chosen this to be my main factor because of my previous experience with that language (see Profile page). In every of those companies, I would want to work as a software engineer. The SE designs, code and debugs software, maintain, upgrade the existing software, train and supervises interns and others. This way I can combine my passion for coding and teaching.

Nuance

Nuance Communications, Inc. (Nuance) is a technology pioneer with market leadership in conversational AI and ambient intelligence. They are a full-service partner trusted by 77 percent of U.S. hospitals and 85 percent of the Fortune 100 companies worldwide. They’ve pioneered speech technologies, natural language understanding, and machine learning for more than 20 years – and have 3000+ patents to prove it. The company’s culture and solutions are continuously evolving to further the positive impact on the world. The company has employees from more than 24 countries and more 9 thousand employees. They have offices in America, Europe, and Asia pacific. There you’ll work with the brightest minds in the industry. They are committed to giving back to the communities and they donate through the Nuance Cares Program.

I would want to work there because this way I can contribute to a better world. Not only my life would be good with all the benefits that come with working there, but also other people’s not matter financially, medically, and so on.

Microsoft

Microsoft is a company that doesn’t need an introduction. Microsoft is one of the world’s leading computer and software companies. It developed C# to cope with the growing demands of web applications back in the year 2000. As a result, the company uses this programming language for the development of web services, games, and applications. Microsoft’s mission is to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more. The company’s values are respect, integrity, and accountability, which are the most important things between people. Microsoft has offices all over the world and has more than 221 thousand employees.

I would want to work there because this way I can contribute to a better world. Not only my life would be good with all the benefits that come with working there, but also other people’s not matter financially, medically, and so on.

Aristocrat

Aristocrat Leisure Limited (ASX: ALL) is a global gaming content and technology company and mobile games publisher. The company has two operating units: Aristocrat Gaming and Pixel United. The company has over 7 thousand employees. It operates internationally even though the headquarters are in Australia. The company ethics and life are wonderful. They have flexible working, people first and always learning mentality, passion for giving back to the communities, health, and wellbeing on first place and diversity and inclusion.

I want to join this community because of the company ethics and the opportunity to learn more about game development.
